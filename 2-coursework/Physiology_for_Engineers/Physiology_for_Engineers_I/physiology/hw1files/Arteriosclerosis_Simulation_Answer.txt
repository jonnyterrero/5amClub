====================================

## iv. What one parameter would you change in the model to best make the model reflect significant wall stiffening from arteriosclerosis?

ANSWER: **ARTERIAL COMPLIANCE (Csa)**

**Rationale:**
- Arteriosclerosis causes hardening and stiffening of arterial walls
- This reduces the ability of arteries to expand and store blood
- In the Windkessel model, this is represented by DECREASED compliance (Csa)
- Lower compliance = stiffer arteries = higher pressure for same blood volume
- This primarily affects systolic pressure and increases pulse pressure

**Parameter to Change:**
- **Csa (Arterial Compliance)** - DECREASE from normal value
- Normal: Csa = 0.0015 L/mmHg
- Arteriosclerosis: Csa = lower values (e.g., 0.0010, 0.0008 L/mmHg)
